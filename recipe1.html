Egg Bake

Recipe Ingredients

Eggs
6 eggs, beaten.
Cottage Cheese
Most brands work here, but I will specifically be using 8 oz low-fat cottage cheese.
Shredded Cheddar
6 oz fat-free cheddar cheese. Using a normal kind of cheese will increase calories.
Turkey Sausage
1/2 pound of cooked sausage, crumbled. You can find a tube of this at Target!
Bell Peppers
Choose 2 peppers of any colors you like, I am using orange and yellow. These will be chopped.
Onion
One medium yellow onion, chopped.
Low Calorie Spray
I am using Pam, but any off brand will work. Spray baking pan before use as well as for sauteing.
Spices
1 tsp each of salt, pepper, onion powder, and garlic powder.

Begin by preheating your oven to 400 degrees.

Beat the eggs with cottage cheese in a large bowl.

Next, saute your peppers and onions in the same pan you cooked your turkey sausage. Spray pan with cooking spray. Cook until onions are translucent and peppers are fragrant.

Mix in your sausage, shredded cheese, and cooked peppers and onions into your egg mixture. Add spices.

Spray baking pan with cooking spray. Pour mixture into pan. Cover with foil.

Let your egg bake cook, covered, for 45 minutes. For the last 15, remove foil. Once the egg bake is no longer jiggly, it is done. Enjoy!

Macros for 6 Servings: Calories - 250, Protein - 20g, Carbs - 10g, Fat - 15g.
